About Google Shop

Google is not just about search, but it also offers a powerful platform for target marketing which can drive a lot of traffic to your Odoo website and lead to higher conversions. Google product feeds or Google shopping is one of the most effective advertising tools present at your disposal. The module is designed to allow you to create products feeds for your Odoo with your Google Merchant Center so you can send the product information from Odoo to your Google Merchant account.

Additionally, map the Odoo Product fields with that of Google fields to display product price, description, etc. correctly on Google shopping.Originally, the service listed prices submitted by merchants and was monetized through AdWords advertising like other Google services. However, Google announced that the service would shift in late-2012 to a paid model where merchants would have to pay the company in order to list their products on the service.

What is Google Shopping?

Prerequisites to configure Odoo Google Product module with Google Merchant Console

To configure the module successfully, you would need the following:

  1. A Google Developer Console account. This account is needed to obtain client Id and client secret for the configuration. Click Me to create a Google Developer console account
  2. A Google Merchant Account to get merchant ID. Google merchant account is also needed to sync your and upload your product details from Odoo. CLICK HERE to create a new account. Then paste the merchant ID in Odoo under account menu. Follow the steps below to verify your website.

Setting up the module

Generating Client ID and Client Secret on Google Developer console.

  1. Once you have set up your Google Dev console account, click on credentials >> OAuth Client ID >> Web application.
  2. Now, choose any name for the configuration.
  3. Enter your website URL under field -’Authorized JavaScript origins’
  4. next, copy the callback URL from account menu in Odoo and paste it under the field - ‘Authorized redirect URIs’
  5. The client ID and client secret is automatically generated.
  6. Once you have obtained the Client ID and Client secret key form the Google dev console, go to your Odoo backend.
  7. Paste the client ID and Secret key in account menu under Google product.

Verifying your Website with Google Merchant Account

  1. If you have logged into your Merchant account for the first time you would need to verify your shop domain with Google.
  2. Go to Business information >> Website.
  3. Enter your website URL and then choose ‘I have access to my server’ option to verify your website. (You can also use any one of the other two options available.)
  4. Then, click on ‘Upload a HTML file on my website’.
  5. Now, download the HTML file which is shown in the page under the steps. For example : google8156543780c743.html
  6. Then, in Odoo, go to account >> Token 1. Here under merchant details. Paste the entire name of the file next to field - ‘URL to verify’.
  7. Now, open the file and paste the text inside it under the field- ‘Data in URL
  8. Go back to your Google merchant account and click on the URL in step 3 on that page. Something like this:- Confirm the upload by visiting https://demo.webkul.com/google8156540c7cf43.html.
  9. Your URL should be verified and you can start to send and sync product information to Google Shopping.
